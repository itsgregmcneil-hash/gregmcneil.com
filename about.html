Hi! I'm Greg,

My earliest memories center on being fascinated by technology. I remember making short movies on an early iMac and being totally enthralled when we slowed down a clip from a point-and-shoot camera. My parents like to remind me that my favorite childhood toy was a box of wires—and that I insisted on bringing them to daycare. The curiosity started early, and it never left. I’ve always loved building useful things at the intersection of liberal arts and technology.

Over time, that curiosity became a career connecting engineering, design, and business. I’ve always viewed creativity and systems thinking as two sides of the same coin—one shaping imagination, the other giving it purpose. I love when an idea moves from a whiteboard sketch to something people can actually use.

I’m happiest when I’m creating—designing a product or service, filming a video, playing music, or helping someone sharpen an idea. I enjoy contributing through mentorship, product development, and collaborations where ideas come to life. If you're working on something interesting, let’s talk.
